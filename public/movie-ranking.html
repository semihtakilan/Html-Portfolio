The Best Movies According to Semih

My top 3 movies of all-time.

G.O.R.A.

Tüccarlik yapan iyi niyetli Arif, uzaylilar tarafindan kaçirilir.
Tutsak olan Arif kaçip dünyaya dönmek ister, fakat karsisinda dünyalilardan nefret eden Komutan Logar vardir.

21

Inspired by real events and people, 21 is about six MIT students who become trained to be experts in card counting in Black Jack
and subsequently took Vegas casinos for millions in winnings.

The Prestij

After a tragic accident, two stage magicians in 1890s London engage in a battle to create the ultimate illusion
while sacrificing everything they have to outwit each other.
